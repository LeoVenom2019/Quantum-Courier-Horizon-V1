# QCH Visual QA Checklist

## Dashboard

- Check sidebar/tabs remain reachable.
- Confirm counters and compact labels do not overflow.
- Verify modal layers sit above dashboard panels and can close.

## Battle

- Confirm background/video loads.
- Confirm player/enemy sprites render and face the intended direction.
- Check projectile, explosion, laser, and overlay effects for blank layers.
- Watch for layout shifts when HP, alerts, or victory/defeat states change.

## Assets

- Confirm images are not broken icons.
- Confirm videos advance beyond a static poster frame.
- Confirm audio controls do not throw autoplay or missing-path errors.

## Responsive

- Check at least one desktop viewport and one mobile viewport.
- Look for clipped buttons, unreadable small text, and overlapping absolute-positioned effects.
